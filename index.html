About us

We are passionate pet people who put our hearts into what we do. We provide a safe healthy environment and safe products for all pets in our store. Our store provides many different services such as Grooming, In Store Vet, Training, Adopting Services and many more.

Our Mission

Our #1 priority is to have our pets walk out happier than they walked in. We offer many types of pet food from kibble to more healthy alternatives located in our fridge sections. You and your pet wont be disappointed with the quality of our BlueDog Dog Food and other products. We donate all funds from our official BlueDog Dog Food to the Ohio State Shelter. To find more information about donating to Our Mission go to PetParadise.com/BlueDogMission.
